THE APPEARENCE OF DISAPPEARANCE
2016

The projects explores different ways of printing and erasing, to/by dissect(ing) the techniques in print. A tension arises of what is absent and present, explore the contra-dictionary of absence: What is erased becomes even more pronounced — the appearance of disappearance.

GRADUATION SHOW CAMPAIN 2016
2016

Designing the graduation show 2016 campaign for the Art Academy Gerrit Rietveld. Involving for the design for the posters, date announcement, invites, merchandise and a video. Together in collaboration with Medeina Musteikyte and Marie Hoejlund.

MEMOIRS TO BUTTONS
2015

A collection where the situation reveals more then content or appearance of the image. Playing with a daily life element developing that makes it’s nostalgic colors shapes and contain on the verge of disappearance. The audience is left to imagine what those images might have looked like.

MICKEY MOUSE IS SICK TODAY
2016

An experiment where we are interacting with the process of offset printing. Pushing the machine to it’s limits to create a various outcome within one design. In collaboration with Sara Hardegger
